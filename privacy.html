Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

At Health Tools Hub, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and health calculators.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through our contact form). Our calculators process data locally in your browser and do not store or transmit personal data to our servers.

2. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect non-personal information about how you interact with our website. This includes data such as pages visited, time spent on the site, and your device’s IP address. Cookies help us improve our services, optimize user experience, and provide relevant advertisements. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services

Our website may include third-party services, such as Google AdSense for advertisements. These third parties may use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior for personalized ads. Please review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more details.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information you provide. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security, and we are not responsible for unauthorized access beyond our control.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to opt out of cookies and tracking through your browser settings. If you provide personal information via our contact form, you may request access, correction, or deletion of that data by contacting us at healthtoolshub@gmailcom.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at healthtoolshub@gmailcom.
