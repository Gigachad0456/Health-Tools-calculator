About Health Tools Hub

Welcome to Health Tools Hub, your go-to resource for free, user-friendly health calculators designed to empower you to make informed decisions about your wellness. Our mission is to provide accurate, evidence-based tools to help you understand your health metrics and take steps toward a healthier lifestyle.

Our calculators cover essential health aspects, including Body Mass Index (BMI), Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), pregnancy due date estimation, daily water intake, and calories burned from steps. Each tool is designed with simplicity and accessibility in mind, offering personalized suggestions and visual charts to enhance your experience.

We are committed to promoting health education and encouraging proactive health management. Our tools are for informational purposes only and are not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.

Thank you for choosing Health Tools Hub. We’re here to support your journey to better health!
